:not()

The :not() functional pseudo-class matches elements that do not match the selectors in its argument.

Status

Baseline Widely Available (since 2023-07-21)

MDN documentation

  • :not()

Specifications

  • Selectors Level 4 (#negation), from Cascading Style Sheets (CSS) Working Group (W3C).
